# NDIS Provider Pro - Complete Project Specification

## Project Overview

**Project Name**: NDIS Provider Pro  
**Framework**: Python/Django  
**Target Market**: NDIS Service Providers in Australia  
**Project Type**: Comprehensive Practice Management Platform  
**Timeline**: 18 months  
**Team Size**: 3-5 developers  

## Executive Summary

NDIS Provider Pro is a comprehensive practice management platform designed specifically for NDIS service providers in Australia. The platform addresses the major pain points providers face including administrative overhead, financial management, compliance requirements, and participant coordination.

## Business Objectives

1. **Reduce Administrative Overhead**: Target 20-30% reduction in administrative time
2. **Improve Financial Management**: Better cash flow and NDIS billing compliance
3. **Enhance Compliance**: Automated quality and safeguarding compliance
4. **Streamline Operations**: Integrated workflow management across all provider functions
5. **Increase Revenue**: Better efficiency leads to more participants and services
