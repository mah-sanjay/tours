## Risk Management

### High-Risk Areas
1. **NDIS Compliance Complexity**
   - Risk: NDIS rules are complex and change frequently
   - Mitigation: Regular consultation with NDIS experts, flexible architecture

2. **Data Security & Privacy**
   - Risk: Healthcare data requires strict security measures
   - Mitigation: Enterprise-grade security, regular security audits

3. **Integration Complexity**
   - Risk: Multiple system integrations increase complexity
   - Mitigation: Phased integration approach, thorough testing

4. **Performance at Scale**
   - Risk: System may not handle large numbers of users
   - Mitigation: Performance testing, optimization, scalable architecture

5. **Regulatory Changes**
   - Risk: NDIS regulations may change during development
   - Mitigation: Flexible design, regular regulatory monitoring

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
- **Regular Risk Assessment**: Monthly risk review meetings
- **Contingency Planning**: Backup plans for critical risks
- **Expert Consultation**: Regular consultation with NDIS experts
- **Phased Development**: Reduce risk through incremental delivery
- **Comprehensive Testing**: Thorough testing at each phase
